Groondlings

Pet and People Projects — Comfort, Craft, and Community

About

Groondlings began as Pet and People Furniture — modular pet steps, cabinetry, and comfortable seating. As our workshop expanded into upholstery, CNC, laser, and welding, we grew into Pet and People Projects. Today we create comfort-forward builds for pets and people, from functional furniture to creative small-batch designs. Powered by Solyd Solutions.

Product Lines

Pet Steps

Modular Baltic-birch steps with cork pads and optional rare-earth magnets. Designed for stability, accessibility, and repairability.

Column Lamps

Baltic-birch columns with hardwood accents, epoxy-ready, and built for serviceable lighting cores.

Upholstery

Vintage rehabs and new builds using resilient foam, textiles, and webbing. Pattern digitization planned for future open templates.

Cabinetry

Practical, modular cabinetry for small spaces. Jig-driven repeatability with careful material and hardware selection.

Craft and Capability

We rely on digital workflows for precision. Deploying a Shapeoko Pro 5 will increase throughput, reduce variance, and expand our ability to prototype fixtures and jigs in-house.

  • Improved nesting for material efficiency
  • Faster jig and fixture production
  • Repeatable tolerances across batches

Operations are supported by Solyd Solutions for CAD/CAM, toolpaths, and analytics. This integration reduces waste, shortens lead times, and documents processes for training and quality control.

Operations and Impact

Small-Batch Production

Batch sizes tuned to demand. Modular designs enable repair, upgrades, and customization.

Community and Training

Pathways in upholstery, CNC, and laser for local makers. Documentation is accessible and repeatable.

Why the Shapeoko Pro 5 Matters

Adding a Shapeoko Pro 5 enables precise joinery for pet steps and cabinetry, rapid fixture development for upholstery templates, and reliable production of lamp components. It directly supports our revenue strategy and deepens the documentation and analytics that Solyd Solutions publishes.
